### What is Career+?
Career+ is an AI-powered resume optimization platform that helps job seekers create ATS-friendly resumes.

### Is it free?
Yes, the MVP version is completely free. Premium features coming soon.

### Do you store my resume?
No, all data is stored locally in your browser using IndexedDB. Nothing is sent to our servers except for AI processing.

### What file formats are supported?
PDF, DOCX, and TXT files up to 10MB.

## ðŸ¤– AI Features

### Which AI models do you use?
- llama3.1:8b for insights
- gemma3:4b for rewriting
- all-MiniLM-L6-v2 for embeddings

### How accurate is the ATS score?
Our ATS scoring is based on industry best practices and common ATS systems. It's a good indicator but not a guarantee.

### Can I trust the AI suggestions?
AI suggestions are helpful but should be reviewed. You have full control to accept or reject changes.

### Why is AI processing slow?
AI models require significant computation. We're working on optimizing response times.
